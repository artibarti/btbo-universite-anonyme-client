Generate Invite Codes
You can generate persistent invite codes, invite codes with expiration date or with a limitation of maximum usage.

Persistent invite code
Persistent invite codes are useful if welcome anyone in your course who get this code.

Invite codes with usage limitation
Invite codes with usage limitation are useful is you want to allow only a limited amount of people into your courses.



Invite codes with expiration
Invite codes with expiration are useful when you want people to join your course only in a specified period.
